A Gimlet is a simple three ingridient drink originating from Admiral Sir Thomas Gimlette in the 19th century, a naval doctor who allegedly invented the drink to improve the taste of the lime juice that sailors had to drink to prevent scurvy. #scurvy<3

Making a Gimlet

Combine two and a half ounces of gin, half an ounce of fresh lime juice, and sugar or a simple syrup of your choice in a shaker with ice. Shake well, strain and serve in a coupe glass garnished with a lime.
